Our Equipment

Hockey essentials: helmet, shoulder pads, gloves, elbow pads, jersey, jock/jill shorts, hockey pants, shin guards, skates, hockey socks, stick. Optional: mouthguard, neck guard, puck, stickhandling aids. Prioritize safety and regularly check equipment for wear.

Hockey Training

Effective hockey training involves a combination of on-ice drills focusing on skills like skating, shooting, and passing, along with off-ice conditioning for strength, agility, and endurance. Coaches emphasize game strategy, teamwork, and individual improvement to enhance overall player performance

Private Lesson

Private hockey lessons offer personalized instruction, refining individual skills such as skating techniques, puck control, and positioning to elevate a player's performance.

Ski Touring

Ski touring is unrelated to hockey. Ski touring involves skiing in the backcountry, often on unmarked or ungroomed trails, while hockey is a team sport played on ice with skates and a puck.
